How a Solar Grid Brought Light to Dharnai Village

In the village of Dharnai, the villagers had no reliable electric network. They used kerosene lamps and diesel generators. One morning, the village woke up to reliable electric network. The Greenspace India, teaming up with BASIX and CEED, built a solar powered micro-grid with 100 kw capacity. With lights in the evening to ensure continuous power, students were able to study after dark and women felt safe outside...
